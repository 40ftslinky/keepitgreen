Pursuing good projects and ideas.

Keep it Green is an initiative that aims to pursue projects and ideas of benefit to our society. In particular, the concerning environmental issues we currently face.

We are asking for your help to fund a project to tackle the waste issue caused by disposable coffee cups with a device to significantly increase the use of reusable coffee cups and that makes using reusable cups convenient, hygienic, easy and fun.

This project has input from a small group of dedicated individuals with a combined experience in product design and development, Fintech, sales and marketing, ethical business practices and consumer insight.
